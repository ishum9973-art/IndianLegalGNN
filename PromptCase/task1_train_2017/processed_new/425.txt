unconstitutional.Article 371-D was introduced in the Constitution by the Constitution (Thirty- Second Amendment) Act 1973, which came into force with effect from 1st July, 1974, and pursuant to c1.(3) thereof the President of India made an order on 19th May, 1975 constituting a, Administrative Tribunal for the State of Andhra Pradesh with jurisdiction to deal with service matters specified in that cl.(5).Allowing the Petitions, HELD: (1) Clause (5) of Art.371-D of the Constitution along with the Proviso is declared to he unconstiutional and void.The Government of India is directed to ensure
