misconduct, as contemplated by the Standing Order 27(1) as applicable to the appellant.Agreeing with the findings, the General Manager of Respondent No.1 passed an order on May 2, 1968, dismissing the appellant from service.Since, however, an industrial dispute was pending, the General Manager directed the appellant to take his final dues together with one month,s pay in lieu of notice and made an misconceived; that since there was no rational connection between the employment of the appellant and Shyam Singh in regard to the affairs of the D.C.M.Chemical Works, Standing Order 27(1) was not
