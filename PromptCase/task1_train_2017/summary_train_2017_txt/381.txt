 Appeal against conviction of appellant for murder of one Lahu Vithu Patil on the night between May 23, and 24, 1960 at village Pasarde . Four persons Rama Krishna  On March 17, 1960, Rama Krishna Patil accused No. 1 and appellant got a knife prepared by Nanu Santu Sutar P.W.7 from a crowbar  An accomplice should be a competent witness against an accused person, s. 133 of the Evidence Act . But the courts cannot lose sight of the rule of prudence and practice . The  Appeal allowed (1) [1916] 2.658 . We, therefore, allow this appeal, set aside the order of conviction and direct that the appellant be released forthwith .
