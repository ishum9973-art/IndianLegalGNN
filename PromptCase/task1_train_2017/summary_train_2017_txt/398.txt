He then went in search of the appellant Sadhai and arrested him  The High Court did not consider the evidence of alibi at all in its judgment . The Sub Inspector deposed that Sadhai was arrested at about 10 p.m. and not at  Sadhai's instigation was likely to result in the kind of injury which was caused to the deceased .
