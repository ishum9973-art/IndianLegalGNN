 The respondents defaulted in the payment demanded, their shares were forfeited under Art. 30 . The respondents filed an application under s. 155 praying that the forfeitures may be set aside  The board of directors of the Company passed a resolution calling the unpaid amount of Rs. 25/- on each share . On January 2, 1957, a call notice was issued to the shareholders  The amount of expenses incurred by the Company by reason of the non-payment was not disclosed . The respondents were not informed how much they should pay on account of the expenses .
