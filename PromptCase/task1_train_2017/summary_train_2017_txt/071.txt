Charges have to be proved beyond reasonable doubt and alleged contemnor becomes entitled to the benefit of doubt .  The High Court held that power of the High Court even under Article 215 of the Constitution has to be exercised in accordance with the procedure prescribed by law . For enforcing the order passed by the  Court suo motu motu initiated contempt proceedings against Tej Veer Singh . The case should not rest only on surmises and conjectures .
