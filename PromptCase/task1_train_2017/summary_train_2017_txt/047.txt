 The respondent -- M/s. Master Construction Company (for short, `the contractor') -- was awarded a contract (CA No. CEBTZ--14/95-96)  The Chief Justice of the Punjab and Haryana High Court decided the application filed by the contractor 4 under Section 11(6) of the 1996 Act and passed the order impugned  The controversy presented before us does not concern the existence of arbitration agreement but it relates to whether after furnishing `no-claim certificates' and the receipt of payment of final bill, as  Courts will not refer any subsequent claim or dispute to arbitration, courts say . Parties to a contract confirm in writing that the contract has been fully and finally discharged by performance of all obligations .  The impugned order dated December 8, 2006 passed by the Chief Justice of the High Court of Punjab and Haryana is set aside . The parties shall bear their own costs .
