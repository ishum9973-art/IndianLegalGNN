 Appeal by special leave from the judgment and order dated May 3, 1967 of the Patna High Court in Criminal Appeal No. 162 of 1967 . The High Court set aside the acquittal  In appeal the learned District Judge, Shahbad acquitted the appellants . He felt unable to rely on the prosecution case for three different reasons . The High Court differing from the 1st appellate  The High Court set aside the judgment of the learned single judge of the High Court and restore that of the Sessions Judge .
