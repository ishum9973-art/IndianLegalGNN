 Appellants were tried by the Fourth Addl. Sessions Judge, Raipur, alleging that they caused the death of one Ajeet . The prosecution case was that on 19  In [?CITATION?], the accused caused serious injuries to the deceased with the handle of a pump due to which the deceased died 3 days thereafter . The motive of the crime  Prosecution must establish, quite objectively, that a bodily injury is present and the nature of injury must be proved . It must establish that there was an intention to inflict that particular bodily injury  Both the appellants had brutally attacked the deceased . The appellants have been rightly convicted under Section 302 IPC . We see no merit in these appeals, which are dismissed .
