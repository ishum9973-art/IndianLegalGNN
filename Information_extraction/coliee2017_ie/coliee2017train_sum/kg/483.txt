 Appeal by special leave for an interlocutory order dated August 25, 1983 passed by the Calcutta High Court . Appeal was delivered by SEN, Juriuri Siliguri,  The High Court should not grant any stay of recovery of tax save under very exceptional circumstances . The grant of stay in such matters, should be an exception and not a rule .  There shall be no order as to order the High Court to refund the difference between the revised and the old rates within three months of the petition being allowed .
