(defendant No.10 in the Delhi Suit), which came to registered as Civil Appeal No.577 of 1987 after the grant of special leave, and the other, by Naresh Kumar Barti, the plaintiff in the Delhi Suit, which came to be registered as Civil Appeal No.578 of 1987 after the grant of special leave.
