In April 2005, the applicant was repatriated to Canada. The applicant challenged the CDS's decision to send him back to Canada by filing an application for judicial review.  II. Applicant's Grievances  The applicant challenges the CDS's decision by submitting three grievances.  Grief 1 - Declaration of the recommendations of the Grievance Board. The applicant's grievance was that the actions taken against him by the commander of the Régiment de Maisonneuve could only be characterized as a disciplinary action and not as an administrative action for insubordination.  The CDS noted that the applicant had already received a recorded warning for professional shortcomings on five occasions during his military career, including two of those warnings being for insubordination.
